Game variables (show)
Move speed: (higher numbers make you move faster; if it's lower than gravity, you can't lift off)
Crash speed: (higher numbers let you land at higher speeds -- this makes it easier to land)
Gravity: (higher numbers make it harder to fly upwards, and pull you to the ground faster)
Rotate speed: (higher numbers make you spin faster -- easier to change direction, harder to land straight up)
Fuel use: (higher numbers use your fuel up more quickly -- lowering this number effectively gives you "more fuel")
